Welcome to WhatsAroundTheCorner!

As first-year students, we struggled to find reliable information about nearby places on campus. Most platforms were either outdated or hard to use, making even simple tasks frustrating. That’s why we built WhatsAroundTheCorner—a platform designed to make exploring campus easier for everyone.

With WhatsAroundTheCorner, shop owners can showcase their products, menus, and prices in a clear and accessible way. Users can explore, compare, and buy based on accurate details and locations. Shop owners can also update their listings to keep information fresh and relevant.

Why Choose Us?

  • A simple, user-friendly platform designed for SRM students and staff.
  • Easily find nearby shops, services, and products—all in one place.
  • Shop owners can reach more customers and keep their listings up to date.
  • Smart filters help users find what they need based on categories, prices, and distance.
  • A personal chatbot is always available to assist with queries, from finding the best deals to recommending nearby stores.
